FYP with SingHealth

Demo See more

Final Year Project (FYP) with SingHealth

Administrators in SingHealth currently face inefficiencies in tracking and maintaining resident doctors' data, resulting in heavy workloads and mismatched records. They may also struggle to identify at-risk residents who may not complete their requirements until it is too late. To address these challenges, my team and I for our final year project (FYP) have created a web application that centralizes SingHealth residents' data. It eliminates the need for data transfer between departments, streamlines workflows with custom functions, and provides a dashboard for increased visibility into residents' progress and non-compliance. This application optimizes data management and facilitates early identification of at-risk residents, improving the overall efficiency and effectiveness of the residency program.

Vendor Management System

My team and I developed a web application called Vendor Management System for IS442, which offers customizable forms with suggestive input, signature, form export, and a user-friendly UI/UX optimized for Quantum Leap. The purpose of the application is to address the difficulties that Quantum Leap faces when onboarding vendors and subcontractors, as well as collecting and retaining necessary forms for record-keeping. Our Vendor Management System simplifies the vendor management process, streamlines onboarding, and ensures prompt notification of any updates or changes through email. The customizable forms, along with form export and signature functionalities, improve record-keeping and compliance. Ultimately, the Vendor Management System enhances the overall experience for both Quantum Leap and their vendors/subcontractors. We received an A for this project.
